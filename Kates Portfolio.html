About

Welcome, my name is Kaitlyn Thomas and I am a junior developer at Eleven Fifty Academy. I have had a deep love for technology and art since middle school and really started to get into art and design after receiving my first graphics tablet for my birthday one year. I began to explore Photoshop and many other Adobe products and quickly became proficient with those programs. I pay strict attention to user experience as well as the overall appearance for all of my projects.


Background

I grew up around technology as well as medicine. My dad has always been in the IT field and worked at Microsoft for many years and my mom is a RN who later worked at a company that created software for fertility and vein clinics. I worked in the veterinary industy as a technician in both general practice and emergency medicine for roughly 2 years after realizing that I did not want to be a physician. I chose to pursue my interest in coding because I love a good challenge and enjoy having the ability to create something out of nothing. The ability to create and application/website/program from nothing is absolutely fascinating to me and I wanted to be a part of this ever-changing field. I love to learn and hone my skills so coding was a great fit for me!
